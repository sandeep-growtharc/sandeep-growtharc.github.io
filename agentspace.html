A Comparative Look at Core Features

AgentSpace is emerging as a powerful platform for building and deploying enterprise-grade AI agents. This interactive overview explores three core aspects: how agents are created, how they connect to data sources, and how they communicate.

1. Agent Creation

No-Code Simplicity & Developer Power

AgentSpace caters to all skill levels by offering two primary methods for creating agents. Explore the approaches below to see which fits your needs, from rapid prototyping with natural language to deep customization with code.

2. Agent Connections

A Rich Ecosystem of Data

An agent's true power comes from the data it can access. AgentSpace achieves this through a robust system of connectors, enabling agents to reason over a wide range of first-party and third-party data sources.

3. Communication

From User Interaction to Agent Collaboration

Effective communication is key. AgentSpace facilitates seamless interaction between users and agents, and is pioneering an open standard for agents to collaborate with each other, unlocking complex, automated workflows.

In summary, AgentSpace provides a flexible and powerful environment for creating, connecting, and communicating with AI agents. The platform's no-code and developer-friendly options for agent creation, its extensive library of data connectors, and its forward-looking approach to agent-to-agent communication make it a compelling choice for enterprises looking to leverage the power of AI.
